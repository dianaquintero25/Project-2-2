We are a bakery located in Sugarland, CA, specializing in pasteries and baked goods. Our ingredients are made with high quality grains and fruits from your local farmers. Our team works hard to bake, serve, and cater to our customers, by making sure we give the best quality and consistent service.

We offer gluten-free, halal meats, vegan, and many other options for any dietary restrictions you may have. Feel free to ask us about our sugar-free options too! Have any questions? Give us a call or walk in and we will be happy to assist you.

Le Bakery + Cafe is a small local business that has partnered with charities to help make a difference. Your business to us matters, and we want to make sure we show that.
